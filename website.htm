Our Raspberry Jam Event
This gives us golden opportunity to interact with young innovative minds having the potential to explore Robotics and AI. Many of us want to know about the construction and working of robots and other artificial intelligent systems. But we do not get much opportunities to learn everything about it. So, we here at GCET, decided to start a program where we can invite you all to participate in the program to learn everything you need to develop your own IT solutions. We will be extremely very happy to see you in the program where we all will try to learn and explore Robotics and physical computing in depth. Come forward and be a part of this rapidly growing opensource community of all the learners, developers and enthusiasts.
